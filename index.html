A Glimpse into My World

Welcome to my personal website. Here you'll discover my passions, cherished memories, and the adventures that shape my life. From childhood bike rides in pursuit of the ice cream man to crafting tamales with my family during Christmas, these moments have defined my journey.

My love for Disneyland, the magic of exploring new places, and the joy of simple pleasures like hikes and crafts, reflect my belief in finding happiness in every moment. As an advocate for children and animals, my career in social work is a testament to my commitment to making a difference.

"Animals are a gift from above for they truly define the words unconditional love."

This quote resonates deeply with me, as my life's philosophy is grounded in kindness, the pursuit of dreams, and the importance of mental health. My journey from sunny California to the University of Washington has been filled with challenges, learning, and growth, leading me to dream of a future where I can create a sanctuary for farm animals and shelter dogs.

I hope this site offers you a window into my world, inspires you to cherish every moment, and reminds you of the beauty of unconditional love that our furry friends teach us.
